GlobeTrek Travel Site Plan

Site Name

GlobeTrek Travel

The name “GlobeTrek” combines “globe” and “trek,” representing global exploration and adventure. It reflects a modern travel brand focused on discovering destinations around the world.

Site Purpose

GlobeTrek Travel is a website designed to help users explore travel destinations around the world. It provides information about cities, attractions, weather, travel tips, and images. Users can browse destinations, filter results, save favorites, and view detailed information through interactive features.

Scenarios

  • What are the best destinations to visit in summer or winter?
  • What attractions are available in a specific country or city?
  • How can I save and organize my favorite travel destinations?

Color Scheme

  • #0D3B66 - Header, navigation, and headings
  • #FAF0CA - Background and content sections
  • #F4D35E - Buttons and highlights
  • #333333 - Text color

Typography

  • Arial - Body text
  • Sans-serif - Headings and navigation
